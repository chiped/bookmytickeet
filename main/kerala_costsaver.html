S3 - KERALA KANYAKUMARI - COST SAVER

9 Days 8 Hotel Nights

 

Visit : Cochin 2N, Alleppey, Kottayam, Kaladi, Munnar 2N, Periyar 1N, Trivandrum 2N, Kovalam, Kanyakumari 1N.

Print
Day 01 : Cochin
Arrive at Cochin - Ernakulam Junction, report to coach and proceed to the hotel. Free time for shopping. Enjoy live Kathakali dance performance in the evening.


Day 02 : Cochin - Kaladi
Visit St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue and Dutch Palace. Take a Harbour Cruise Ride to see the Chinese Fishing Nets. Excursion to Kaladi to visit Adi Shankaracharya's birth place.


Day 03 : Cochin - Munnar
Proceed to Munnar. Arrive at Munnar in the evening.


Day 04 : Munnar
Enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam. View Tea Garden, visit Echo Point and Eravikulam (Rajamalai) National Park.

Day 05 : Munnar - Periyar (Thekkady)
Arrive at Periyar. Free time for shopping at Kumily Spice Village. Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary by boat in Periyar Lake.

Day 06 : Periyar - Backwaters ride - Trivandrum
Proceed to Trivandrum. En-route enjoy the famous Kerala Backwater ride from Nedumudi to Alleppey. Arrive at Trivandrum in the evening.

Day 07 : Trivandrum - City Tour
Visit Padmanabh Swami temple, Raja Ravi Varma Art Gallery, Napier Museum, Velli Garden and have fun at Kovalam Beach. Enjoy get-together with your travel companions in the evening.

Day 08 : Trivandrum - Kanyakumari
Arrive at Kanyakumari. Take a Ferry ride to Swami Vivekanand Rock Memorial to see the meditation hall and smarak. Visit Kanyakumari Mandir, Tri-Samudra Milan and Gandhi Mandapam.

Day 09 : Departure from Kanyakumari
Board the train for your hometown. Tour Concludes.
