S1 - KERALA KANYAKUMARI - CLASSIC

10 Days 9 Hotel Nights

 

 

Visit: Cochin 2N, Munnar 2N, Periyar 1N, Kumarakom 1N, Alleppey, Kanyakumari 1N, Trivandrum, Kovalam 2N.

Print
Day 01 : Cochin
Arrive at Cochin Airport. Report to coach and proceed to the Hotel. Enjoy live Kathakali dance performance in the evening.

Day 02 : Cochin - City Tour
Visit St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue and Dutch Palace. Take a Harbour Cruise Ride to see the Chinese Fishing Nets. Have a delicious lunch at Taj Malabar 5 star hotel. Free time for shopping.

Day 03 : Cochin - Munnar
Proceed to Munnar. En-route view Tea Garden. On arrival free time for relaxation in the evening.

Day 04 : Munnar
Visit Echo Point and Eravikulam (Rajamalai) National Park. Enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam.

Day 05 : Munnar - Periyar
Proceed to Periyar. On arrival free time for shopping at Kumily Spice Village. Enjoy Kairali show in the evening.

Day 06 : Periyar - Kumarakom
Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary by boat in Periyar Lake. Proceed to Kumarakom.

Day 07 : Kumarakom - Kanyakumari
Enjoy the famous Kerala Backwaters ride at Kumarakom. Proceed to Kanyakumari. Arrive at Kanyakuamri in the evening.

Day 08 : Kanyakumari - Kovalam
Take a ferry ride to Swami Vivekanand Rock Memorial. Visit the meditation hall and the smarak. Visit Kanyakumari Mandir, Tri Samudra Milan and Gandhi Mandapam. Proceed to Kovalam. En-route visit Kanyakumari Wax Museum. Arrive at Kovalam in the evening.

Day 09 : Kovalam - Trivandrum - Kovalam
Excursion to Trivandrum. Visit Padmanabh Swami Temple, Raja Ravi Varma Art Gallery and Napier Museum. Visit Velli Garden and enjoy speed boat ride at Velli Lake. Enjoy get-together with your travel companions in the evening.

Day 10 : Departure from Trivandrum
Board the flight for your hometown. Tour Concludes.

 

Note :
