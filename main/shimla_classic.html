H1 - SHIMLA MANALI - CLASSIC

9 Days 8 Hotel Nights

 

Visit : Chandigarh 2N, Shimla 2N, Naldehra, Kufri, Kullu, Manali 4N, Rohtang Snow Point, Naggar.


Day 01 : Delhi - Chandigarh
Arrive at Delhi Domestic Airport, report to coach and proceed to Chandigarh.

Day 02 : Chandigarh - Shimla - Mall Road
Proceed to Shimla. En-route visit Pinjore Garden. Arrival at Shimla. In the evening visit the famous Mall road, Scandal Point and Shimla Church.

Day 03 : Shimla - Naldehra - Kufri
Morning free time for relaxation. Later visit Naldehra Golf course, Kufri - Winter Sports Capital, Indira Bungalow and Himalayan Zoo.

Day 04 : Shimla - Manali
Departure to Manali. En-route pass by Pandoh Dam, Beas river, Sutlaj river, Beas Sutlaj Link Project, Kullu Valley. Visit Shawl Factory at Kullu. Arrive at Manali in the evening.

Day 05 : Manali - Local sights
In the morning visit Naggar Art Gallery, which houses rare paintings of Prof. Nicholas K. Roerich and Jagatsukh Gayatri Mandir. Evening explore the ancient temples like Vashista Kund, Manu Mandir and Hidimba Mandir.

Day 06 : Manali - Rohtang Snow Point
Enjoy spectacular drive towards Rohtang snow point. Free time to play and enjoy in the snow. Evening enjoy Himalayan Folk dance.

Day 07 : Manali - Water Rafting - Day at Leisure
Explore the beautiful surroundings of Manali, Enjoy water rafting in Beas river (subject to operation). Free day for shopping or leisure activities. Enjoy get together with your travel companions in the evening.

Day 08 : Manali - Chandigarh
Departure to Chandigarh. En-route visit Vaishnomata Mandir at Kullu. Arrive at Chandigarh in the evening.

Day 09 : Chandigarh - Departure from Delhi
Visit Sukhana Lake, Rose Garden, Rock Garden. Proceed to Delhi. Board the flight for your home town. Tour Concludes.
