If you book the tour within 30 days prior to departure, urgent Visa processing charges of INR 2000 per visa will be applicable.

Balance Payment :

 

You must make the Balance payment towards the tour price (INR and Forex for World tours) 30 days prior to the tour departure.

Late Payment :

 

If we do not receive the entire tour price payable to us 30 days prior to tour departure, we will not be able to process your booking and arrange your visas (in case of World tours). Your registration amount is likely to be forfeited and your booking stands automatically cancelled.
Cheque payments will not be accepted 15 days prior to the tour departure. Any payments made 15 days prior to departure will have to be made by cash / Demand Draft for all tours. For Forex payment only demand draft will be accepted.

One time payment:

 

To avoid frequent visits, you can pay the balance amount at the time of registration through post-dated cheques. If there is any variation in the final bill, the same will be communicated to you and the payment will have to be made by cheque.

Booking from Overseas :

 

Can we join the tour from 'Anywhere in the world'? or Can an NRI or a foreigner join a tour?
Yes, one can join any of our tours. Guests can join the tour from 'Anywhere in the World'. These guests are termed as Joining and Leaving guests, (J&L guests). They can join the tour at the first destination and leave the tour at the last destination.

Joining & Leaving Guests for Indian Tours:
(J&L) Guests must possess Rail/Air tickets for the entire travel including any internal journey as per the tour itinerary. They must make the payment in US$ either at our offices or directly by telegraphic/transfers. Guests will meet the group directly at the reporting place as per the itinerary.

India Tours: NRI guests should pay tour cost defined in USD.

Joining & Leaving Guests for World tours:
These guests must possess air tickets for the entire travel including any internal flights, insurances and visas as per the tour itinerary. They must make the payment in US$/Euro either at our offices / directly by telegraphic transfer.
The cost of air travel including internal flights, airport tax, departure tax, visa, Insurance and airport transfers are not included in the tour price. These guests will meet the group directly at the reporting place as per the itinerary. Insurance is necessary for Europe, Egypt and Dubai tours. Guests are requested to keep proper communication with our team about respective departures. bookmyticket will not be responsible for any damages due to change in programme due to various reasons.
