K1 - KASHMIR VAISHNODEVI - CLASSIC

9 Days 8 Nights

 

Visit: Srinagar 2N (House-boat), Sonmarg, Gulmarg 2N, Khilanmarg (Affarwat), Avantipur, Pahalgam 2N, Chandanwari, Aru Valley, Katra 2N, Vaishnodevi.

Day 01 : Srinagar
Arrive at Srinagar airport, report to the House Boat.

Day 02 : Srinagar - Sonmarg - Srinagar
Full day excursion to Sonmarg - known as 'Meadow of Gold'. Later visit the carpet factory.

Day 03 : Srinagar - Gulmarg
Enjoy Dal Lake Shikara ride as well as Speed Boat ride and visit Char Chinar Island. Photograph yourself in Kashmiri outfits at Char Chinar. In the afternoon visit the highest point of Srinagar at Shankaracharya hill and the holy temple, followed by the famous Nishat Baug-'The garden of pleasure'. In the evening visit famous Shalimar Bagh-'Abode of love'. Arrive at Gulmarg - known as
'Meadow of Flowers'. En-route view the apple orchards.

Day 04 : Gulmarg - Khilanmarg (Affarwat) - Gulmarg
Excursion to Khilanmarg. Experience the longest Cable Car ride up to the Affarwat snow point. En-route pass by the picturesque golf course. Free time to play and enjoy in the snow.

Day 05 : Gulmarg - Pahalgam
Visit Avantipur ruins, Cricket bat factory and pass-by the Pampore Saffron fields. Enjoy shopping in the evening at Pahalgam.

Day 06 : Pahalgam - Chandanwari - Pahalgam
Proceed to Chandanwari for a magnificent view of Lidder valley. Visit Aru Valley - a fascinating Valley around 15 km from Pahalgam. You can photograph yourself in Kashmiri outfits at various photo studios of Pahalgam. Enjoy get together with your travel companions in the evening.

Day 07 : Pahalgam - Katra - Vaishnodevi
Arrive at Katra, the base point of Vaishnodevi in the evening. Later proceed for the Vaishnodevi Temple, a trek of total 28 kms.

Day 08 : Vaishnodevi - Katra
Arrival from Vaishnodevi. Free day for relaxation or leisure activities.

Day 09 : Katra - Departure from Jammu
Departure to Jammu. Board the flight for your hometown. Tour Concludes.
